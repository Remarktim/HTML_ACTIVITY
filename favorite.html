Little Red Riding Hood

Journey into the heart of the forest with ‘Little Red Riding Hood’. This timeless tale follows a young girl named Red, who sets off to visit her grandmother. Along the way, she encounters a cunning wolf with a deceptive plan. With an enchanting forest as the backdrop, this story unfolds into a captivating adventure filled with suspense, lessons, and the enduring bond of family. Experience the magic and mystery of ‘Little Red Riding Hood’ like never before.

Main Characters

Little Red Riding Hood: A dear little girl who wears a red velvet cap and is loved by everyone.

Grandmother: Little Red Riding Hood’s ailing grandmother who lives deep in the woods.

Wolf:The cunning antagonist who plots to deceive and harm Little Red Riding Hood and her grandmother.
